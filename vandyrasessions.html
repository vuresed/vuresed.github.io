The Mindful BystanderBuilding upon the active bystander incident response skills cultivated in Green Dot training, this session offers participants the opportunity to learn more about why bystander intervention works and its effects multiply when modeled in campus communities. Mindfulness is scientifically proven to produce safer, calmer, more effective crisis response (and reporting of crisis response). Students will learn about the mindfulness-emotional intelligence-bystander intervention connection and leave with two mindfulness strategies (used by Google!) that can be quickly employed to ready active intervention in situations posing risk for sexual harassment and violence.
