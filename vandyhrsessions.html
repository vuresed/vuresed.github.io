Now What? Groundwork for a Successful YearThis session will explore the expectations of an HR and will feature the opportunity to set some goals for the year. 
The Head ResidentExplore practical ways to motivate and address staff while developing a clear understanding of the HR role. Engage in conversations with your peers about the position and expectations of your fellow HRs.
Behind the ScenesExplore the administrative responsibilities of the Head Resident position. Identify ways to balance the HR role and institutional commitments, and vocalize your needs as an HR. 
Returner HR Panel Take the opportunity to tap into the wealth of knowledge that exists within the returning HR pool. Share previous experiences and plan for futurue successes.
Meet the Assistant Director of Care ManagementHear directly from OHARE's new Assistant Director for Care Management, Lauren Moon, about how the new role will function and what it means for HRs and RAs this year.
The Role of the Program ManagerLearn about what the newly created Program Manager role will handle and how it impacts RA responsibilities.
Making the Most of ConflictLearning to use conflict as a positive tool to facilitate stronger connections and relationships.
Community Development Plan IntroductionGet introduced to Community development Plans and the role they serve within OHARE. Become familiar with why they exist and how we go about implementing them to maximize our community development efforts.
Dinner & CDP Review with AC Take time with your Area Coordinator to learn the ins and outs of your own community's CDP over dinner. Brainstorm ways to best engage the RAs with this plan and how to utilize this throughout the year.
Meaningful Conversations (HR Role) Discuss the evolution of Meaningful Conversations and hear about how OHARE plans to make MCs the central component of our community development plans moving forward.
